Get Ready to Board the Reading Express

On July 8, The Tacy Foundation sponsored Claudia Scott in a special event called Reading Express at The Children's Inn as part of NIH's summer camp activity. On July 11th and 13th she was at National Military Medical Center (Walter Reed & Bethesda Naval Center) with children of the Wounded Warriors. Since children from outside the campuses were not allowed at The Children's Inn or Walter Reed, the foundation held an encore presentation of Reading Express at the Germantown Public Library for the local community. An engaging story and activity centers provided an hour of adventure.


Ms. Scott is a teacher on the Navajo Reservation in Chinle, Arizona, where there is no public library. She developed the weekly Reading Express to bring the living art of books, stories, drama, reading and imagination to the Navajo children. Sixty to 100 children regularly attend her story hours. This special event will be recorded for patients in children's hospitals and their families. Believing the imagination has no boundaries, no limits, Ms. Scott invites children into the world of the story from wherever they are.
