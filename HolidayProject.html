Holiday Project

The Holiday Project is created to spread holiday cheer with those who are lonely or recovering from an illness during the holiday season (elderly people in nursing homes, hospital patients, etc.).


A donation of only $10 enables us to give a CD to someone in need. It's also possible to get a copy of the CD when you make a donation that you can give this holiday season. It makes a great gift for family, friends, teachers, bus drivers, and colleagues. Contact the Tacy Foundation to make arrangements. If you'd like to donate online, click here.
